What I've learned

A few things I believe about AI adoption

Models don't matter. Implementation does.

The latest LLM means nothing if it's not woven thoughtfully into the tools and workflows people already use. Integration is everything.

Mindset beats features

How people think about AI matters more than what AI can do. Adoption starts with belief, not a feature list.

Meet people where they are

Most people don't know where to start with AI. The best programs don't just inform. They guide, step by step, in context.

Earn trust by answering "so what?"

Every new tool has to earn its place. That means proving usefulness in someone's real day, not a demo environment.
